# **SUPERMART GROCERY SALES IN PYTHON**


# **Findings Summary**

1. Total sales are strong, indicating healthy revenue generation across all regions and categories.

2. Total orders volume is substantial, showing consistent customer activity and product demand.

3. Profit levels are positive, suggesting overall good cost control and profitable product lines.

4. Average Order Value (AOV) is at a stable level, revealing balanced customer purchasing behavior.

5. Customer base is diverse, with a good number of unique customers contributing to overall sales.

6. Highest sales regions dominate revenue, indicating geographical strengths that can be further leveraged.

7. Sales vary significantly across categories, helping identify top-performing and underperforming product groups.

8. Daily sales trend shows fluctuations, highlighting peak days and potential seasonal or behavioral patterns.

9. Profit by category visualization reveals which categories drive the most profitability, guiding product strategy.

10. Top customers significantly impact revenue, suggesting potential opportunities for loyalty or retention programs.
